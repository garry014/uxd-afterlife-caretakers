    Who can Book?

    We offer counselling services to all our will maker's beneficiaries, just in case you need someone to talk to.

    What are the rates?

    Rates are charged at an hourly basis depending on the counsellor. Take a look at all our counsellor profiles and choose the best one for yourself.

    Where and when are the appointments?

    You are able to choose the date and duration of the appointments, and whether an online or physical appointment would be most comfortable for you.
