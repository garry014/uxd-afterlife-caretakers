How does this work?
1. You complete your pre-funeral planning and opt for future payment.
2. We will send an email to your executors to ensure they know about it.
3. Once your executor acknowledged it, we will keep your plan in our database.
4. Upon your final breath, your executors will make payment on your behalf and we will execute your plan according to your plans.
