Hello, I am Sarah! A liscensed counsellor with a Masters in Health Psychology. I have 3.5 years of experience and have worked with a variety of individual with diverse backgrounds.

My goal as a counsellor is to fully understand my client's perspective and feelings, which will then allow me to help them in the healing process in a way that they're comfortable with.

Sarah has made me realised that I could still continue living a good life even though I had lost my Uncle who I was very close to.

~Kelley, Beneficiary

In my years of experience, I have worked with over 50 clients that faced different issues while grieving. I work with my clients to create an open and safe environment where issues, thoughts, and feelings can be shared without fear of judgment.

I am known for being a good listener, caring and sensitive. I mostly tend to draw from the person-centered approach when in a therapeutic relationship with my clients. I make consistent efforts to meet each client needs and to serve each individual who is progressing through their unique process of self-healing.
